The **Winter Workshop on Complex Systems** is a one-week workshop where young researchers worldwide come together to work on interdisciplinary projects around complex systems.

**The primary focus of the workshop is for participants to engage into novel research projects.**

This is the 10th edition of the WWCS and it will be held in [**Rifugio Madonna delle Nevi**](https://www.madonnadellenevibg.it/) (Lombardy, Italy) from the 26th to the 31st of January 2025.
